Welcome to ImageAI Computer Vision Applications!


Discover the power of computer vision with ImageAI's open-source Python library. ImageAI empowers developers to build applications and systems with self-contained computer vision capabilities. Explore our two web applications below and unleash the potential of image and video recognition in your projects.

Image Prediction

Upload your images and leverage ImageAI's advanced image recognition models to analyze and predict the objects present in the images. With accurate predictions and corresponding probabilities, you can gain valuable insights and enhance your applications.

Video Object Detection

Upload your video and utilize ImageAI's object detection models to analyze and predict the objects present in each frame. Uncover valuable insights and make informed decisions with accurate predictions from your videos. Please note that the processing time of the detection may vary depending on the size of your input video and the detection model you chose. Please wait patiently for the results to be generated.
